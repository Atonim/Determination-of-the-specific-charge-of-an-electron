Instructions for working with the program:

To start the installation, click
on the top of the "POWER" button (the button will light up)
and let the unit warm up for 2-4 minutes.

To change the position of the toggle switches
click on the field where you want to move the slider,
or a word describing the desired value.

Move sliders to change values of
deflection voltage, accelerating voltage
and current strength.
